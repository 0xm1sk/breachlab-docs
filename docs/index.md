# BreachLab Operator Notes

Welcome to the forge. This site is a living document of my progress through the BreachLab series—a training ground designed to build operatives, not just issue certificates.

In this industry, the gap between "knowing a tool" and "knowing the system" is where most people fail. These notes are about closing that gap. I'm documenting the logic, the failures, and the final solves to turn raw experience into a structured knowledge base.

## 📂 Operational Tracks

### [Ghost Track](./ghost/index.md)
**The Foundation.** Linux and Shell fundamentals. This is where the basics of moving through a system, handling shell weirdness, and identifying privilege escalation vectors are mastered.

### [Phantom Track](./phantom/index.md)
**Post-Exploitation.** Everything that happens after the initial foothold. From credential harvesting and persistence to Kubernetes cluster takeovers and cloud pivots.
